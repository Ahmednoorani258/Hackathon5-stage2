# FlowSync — Company Profile

## Overview
- **Company name:** FlowSync, Inc.
- **Founded:** 2022
- **Headquarters:** Remote-first (US/EU time zone coverage)
- **Mission:** Help teams plan, execute, and ship work with clarity—by unifying projects, tasks, and collaboration in one fast, intuitive workspace.

## What We Build
**FlowSync** is a modern team collaboration and project management platform designed for teams that need structure without bureaucracy. FlowSync combines:
- Project planning (projects, milestones, dependencies)
- Execution (tasks, boards, sprints, checklists)
- Visibility (timeline/Gantt, workload views, reports)
- Collaboration (comments, @mentions, file links, notifications)
- Automation (rules, templates, integrations)
- AI assistance (AI Insights for summaries, next steps, and risk signals)

FlowSync’s product philosophy is **“fast to adopt, hard to outgrow.”** Teams can start with a simple board and scale to multi-project portfolios without switching tools.

## Target Customers
FlowSync serves **small to mid-size tech teams, startups, and agencies** typically ranging from **5–200 people**, including:
- Product & engineering teams
- Design and research teams
- Customer success and support teams
- Marketing and growth teams
- Digital agencies managing multiple client projects

**Primary buyer personas:**
- Head of Product / PM Lead
- Engineering Manager
- Operations / Chief of Staff (startups)
- Agency Owner / Delivery Lead

## Pricing & Packaging
FlowSync uses per-user pricing with clear tier differentiation.

### Free
Best for individuals and very small teams exploring the platform.
- Up to **3 projects**
- Basic tasks & boards
- Limited automations
- Community support

### Pro — **$29/user/month**
Best for growing teams that need cross-project visibility and integrations.
- Unlimited projects
- Boards + Timeline/Gantt
- Automations
- AI Insights (standard)
- Integrations (Slack, GitHub, Google Drive, Teams, Zapier)
- Standard support

### Enterprise — **$99/user/month (billed annually)**
Best for organizations needing advanced security, controls, and SLAs.
- SSO/SAML, SCIM (where applicable)
- Advanced permissions and audit trails
- Data residency options (where applicable)
- Custom onboarding & workspace design support
- Priority support + named escalations

> Note: Billing questions, refunds, charge disputes, and contract changes are handled by the Billing/RevOps team and must be escalated per the escalation rules.

## Support Philosophy
FlowSync support is built on three principles:
1. **Resolve first contact whenever possible** (while staying within policy and permissions).
2. **Be transparent** about limitations, timelines, and next steps.
3. **Reduce repeat issues** through documentation, proactive guidance, and product feedback loops.

## Company Values
- **Customers in the loop:** Communicate early, communicate clearly.
- **Own the outcome:** We don’t deflect; we drive issues to resolution.
- **Make it simple:** Prefer straightforward workflows and fewer clicks.
- **Earn trust:** Security, privacy, and reliability are non-negotiable.
- **Ship responsibly:** Fast iteration with respect for user workflows and data.

## Support SLAs (Service Level Commitments)
FlowSync provides support coverage with tiered response targets.

### Response Time Targets
- **Free:** Best-effort, typically within **2 business days**
- **Pro:** Within **12 hours**
- **Enterprise:** Within **1 hour** for high-priority issues; **4 hours** otherwise

### Resolution Targets (Guidelines)
- **P1 / High:** Mitigation or clear action plan within **4 hours**
- **P2 / Medium:** Progress update within **1 business day**
- **P3 / Low:** Progress update within **3 business days**

> Final resolution depends on issue type and customer environment; FlowSync commits to frequent updates and clear ownership.

## 24/7 Digital FTE Vision
FlowSync is building a **Customer Success Digital FTE**: a 24/7 AI employee that can:
- Answer product questions across **Email (Gmail), WhatsApp, and Web Form**
- Identify intent, urgency, and sentiment
- Maintain **cross-channel conversation continuity**
- Execute standard support workflows and guide customers step-by-step
- Escalate policy-sensitive, high-risk, or complex requests with a complete handoff package

The goal is to provide **instant, consistent, human-quality support** for common requests—while ensuring safe escalation for billing, legal, security, and account access issues.
